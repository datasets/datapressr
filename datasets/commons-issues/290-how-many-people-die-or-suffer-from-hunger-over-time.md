# How many people die or suffer from hunger over time?

We want to find out how many people have died or suffered over time worldwide, so we can follow the progress of the global fight against hunger.

## Research

* https://ourworldindata.org/hunger-and-undernourishment :star: 
  * global number of individuals undernourished - 1991-2017
  * share of undernourished population - 2000 - 2016
  * can download csv file
 
* https://ourworldindata.org/famines :star:
  * famine victims values for each decade 
  * 1900 - 2016

* http://www.fao.org/state-of-food-security-nutrition/en/ :star:
  * chart prevalence of undernourishment 
  * 2005 - 2018

* https://www.worldometers.info/
  * constantly growing number of people who died today
  * growing number undernourished people in the world 

* https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/sn.itk.defc.zs?end=2017&start=2000&view=chart
  * for each country most recent year + most recent value
