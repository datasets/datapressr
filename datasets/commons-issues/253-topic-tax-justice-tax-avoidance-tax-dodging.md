# [Topic] Tax Justice / Tax Avoidance / Tax Dodging

Tax Avoidance ("Dodging") by rich companies, especially digital ones like Google, Apple, Amazon, is frequently brought up as both a clear injustice and something that, if corrected, would make a real difference to reducing inequality.[^1]

This issue is about getting the data together on this issue.

## Links

* https://datafortaxjustice.net/

## Questions

### How much money would we gain by stopping tax avoidance

How much money would we gain by stopping tax "dodging"? (And who is we?)

Conversely, how much is it costing countries whether that's e.g. the UK or the US or developing countries?
